SPECIFICATION
Title: Mechanical Seal and Bearing Monitoring Specification — Rotating Equipment
Applicable Equipment: P-101A, P-101B, M-330
Reference Standards: ISO 21049, ISO 10816, OISD-STD-105

This specification sets the site-wide minimum monitoring requirements for
centrifugal pump seals and bearings. Vibration alarm setpoints shall not
exceed Zone B limits per ISO 10816. Seal weep of any kind shall trigger a
non-conformance entry and mandatory replacement scheduling within one
operating cycle, per ISO 21049. Deviations from this specification —
including deferred seal replacement following a logged weep — shall be
escalated to the Plant Manager, not resolved solely at the Maintenance
Engineer level. This specification was issued following review of recurring
seal/bearing degradation patterns on rotating equipment including P-101A.
