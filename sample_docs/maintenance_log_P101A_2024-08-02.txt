MAINTENANCE LOG
Work Order: WO-11675
Date: Aug 2, 2024
Equipment: Pump P-101A (Centrifugal Feed Pump, Unit 3)
Technician Role: Maintenance Engineer

Mechanical seal failure on P-101A during 0400 shift — visible leak and
process fluid spray at seal housing. Immediate breakdown; pump tripped and
isolated per lockout-tagout procedure. Near-miss avoided as no ignition
source was present at time of leak, but incident report filed given the
proximity to hot work area. Standby pump P-101B started successfully with
no interruption to downstream unit. Bearing found scored on teardown,
consistent with the elevated vibration/temperature trend logged since
January 2024. Full bearing and seal cartridge replacement performed under
PTW-55388. Root-cause review requested.
