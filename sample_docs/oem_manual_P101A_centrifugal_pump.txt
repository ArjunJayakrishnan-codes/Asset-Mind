Manufacturer Reference Standard: ISO 5199, ISO 21049

Section 4 — Bearing and Seal Maintenance
Recommended bearing vibration alarm threshold: 2.8 mm/s RMS (Zone B upper
limit per ISO 10816 for this pump class). Recommended bearing temperature
alarm: 65°C. Mechanical seal type: cartridge, single, ISO 21049 compliant.
Seal flush line must maintain minimum 2 bar differential above stuffing box
pressure at all times; any sustained weep at the seal face should be treated
as an early failure indicator, not a cosmetic issue, and scheduled for
replacement within one operating cycle. Recommended PM interval: quarterly
lubrication and vibration check, annual full bearing inspection. Standby
unit (P-101B configuration) should be exercised monthly per OEM guidance.
