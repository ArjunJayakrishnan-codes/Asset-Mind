NEAR-MISS REPORT
Date: Oct 2, 2024
Equipment: Control Valve CV-512
Reported By: Operator
Classification: Valve slow-to-respond (near-miss)

CV-512 was slow to respond to a control-system throttle command during a
process upset, creating a brief pressure deviation downstream of Pump
P-101A. Operator intervened manually and no trip or spill occurred. This
follows the deviation noted in the April 2024 calibration (WO-11720) that
flagged actuator response time as out-of-specification. Recommend
prioritizing the actuator diaphragm inspection rather than deferring to
next shutdown, given the proximity to the P-101A drivetrain that already
has an open reliability concern.
