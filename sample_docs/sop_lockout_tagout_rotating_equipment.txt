STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
SOP: Lockout-Tagout for Rotating Equipment
Applicable Equipment: P-101A, P-101B, M-330, CV-512
Applicable Roles: Maintenance Engineer, Supervisor, Contractor
Reference Standard: Factory Act, OISD-STD-105

All rotating equipment work (pumps, motors, control valves) requires energy
isolation, lock application, and tag placement before any permit to work is
activated. Verification step: Shift Engineer or Supervisor must confirm
zero-energy state before signing off the permit. This procedure applies to
both planned maintenance and breakdown/emergency work, including the P-101A
bearing and seal replacement under PTW-55388 and routine motor work on
M-330. Non-compliance with this SOP has been identified as a contributing
factor in past near-miss events across the site.
