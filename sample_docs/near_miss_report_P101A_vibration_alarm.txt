NEAR-MISS REPORT
Date: Jun 18, 2024
Equipment: Pump P-101A
Reported By: Operator
Classification: High-vibration trip (near-miss)

P-101A tripped on high-vibration alarm during normal operation. Shift
Engineer confirmed no immediate leak or damage visible on walk-down.
Vibration reading at time of trip: 4.1 mm/s RMS, above the alarm setpoint.
This follows an upward trend documented in Q1 and Q2 2024 inspection
reports and the March 2024 maintenance log seal-weep entry. Pump was
restarted after a 30-minute cool-down with no anomaly, but the recurrence of
elevated vibration readings on this asset over multiple months is a
deviation pattern worth flagging for predictive-maintenance review before a
full breakdown occurs.
