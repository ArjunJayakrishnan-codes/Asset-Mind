COMPLIANCE AUDIT REPORT — 2024 Annual Review
Auditor Role: QA Engineer / Inspector
Reference Standards Reviewed: OISD-STD-105, DGMS, Factory Act, PESO,
TIA-942, BICSI, Uptime Institute Tier III, ISO 10816, ISO 21049, ISO 5199

Site-wide compliance audit covering rotating equipment (P-101A, P-101B,
M-330), pressure equipment (V-405, T-210), control systems (CV-512), and
data-center infrastructure (UPS-301, GEN-110, SW-220). All required
standards were found referenced in current documentation with the
exception of a lapsed relief-valve calibration certificate on V-405, which
is being tracked as an open compliance item. The audit specifically notes
that the P-101A seal-failure incident of August 2024 highlighted a gap
between inspection findings (Q1/Q2 2024 deviations) and maintenance
escalation — a process gap rather than a documentation or standards-
coverage gap. Recommend implementing an automated escalation threshold tied
to the ISO 10816 vibration Zone B limit already specified for this
equipment class.
