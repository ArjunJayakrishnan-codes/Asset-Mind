INSPECTION REPORT — Q1 2024
Equipment: Pump P-101A
Inspector Role: QA Engineer
Reference Standard: OISD-STD-105, ISO 10816

Scheduled vibration analysis conducted on P-101A rotating assembly. Outboard
bearing vibration measured at 2.1-2.6 mm/s RMS across the quarter, trending
upward. Minor deviation noted against baseline commissioning data but within
alarm limits. Mechanical seal condition rated "monitor" following a reported
weep in March. Recommend increasing inspection frequency from quarterly to
monthly until vibration trend stabilizes. No non-conformance raised this
quarter; advisory notice issued to Maintenance Engineer.
