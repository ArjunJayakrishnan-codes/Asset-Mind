STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
SOP: P-101A Startup and Shutdown
Applicable Roles: Operator, Supervisor
Reference Standard: OISD-STD-105

1. Confirm suction and discharge valves aligned; confirm standby pump P-101B
   status before starting P-101A.
2. Verify seal flush line pressure within specification before start.
3. Start pump; monitor vibration and bearing temperature for first 15 minutes
   of operation — any reading above 2.8 mm/s RMS or 65°C requires Supervisor
   notification before continuing.
4. For shutdown, ramp down flow gradually; do not deadhead the pump.
5. Log all readings in the maintenance log at each shift handover.
6. Any deviation from normal seal flush pressure or vibration trend must be
   reported immediately, not deferred to the next scheduled inspection.
