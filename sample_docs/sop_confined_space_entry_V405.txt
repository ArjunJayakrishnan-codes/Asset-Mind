STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
SOP: Confined Space Entry — Vessel V-405
Applicable Roles: Safety Officer, Supervisor, Contractor
Reference Standard: Factory Act, PESO

Entry into V-405 for internal inspection or cleaning requires gas testing,
confined-space permit issuance by the Safety Officer, and continuous
attendant supervision. This SOP is cross-referenced in the annual PESO
inspection of V-405 and should be reviewed alongside area hot-work permits
given V-405's proximity to Pump P-101A and the August 2024 seal-failure
incident location.
