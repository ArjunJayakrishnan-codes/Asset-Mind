INCIDENT REPORT
Date: Sep 14, 2024
Equipment: Motor M-330 (Pump Drive Motor, Unit 3)
Classification: Overheat trip / breakdown
Reported By: Shift Engineer
Regulatory Notification: DGMS

M-330 tripped on winding-temperature protection during normal operation,
approximately six weeks after the P-101A seal-failure breakdown on the same
drivetrain. Investigation found no direct link to the pump-side seal
failure, but noted that the coupling misalignment tolerance check performed
in July (WO-11602) should be repeated post-repair as a precaution. Motor
was inspected, allowed to cool, and returned to service with no parts
replacement. Recommend adding M-330 to the predictive-maintenance
vibration-monitoring program alongside P-101A and P-101B.
