MAINTENANCE LOG
Work Order: WO-11208
Date: Mar 22, 2024
Equipment: Pump P-101A (Centrifugal Feed Pump, Unit 3)
Technician Role: Maintenance Engineer

Operator reported slight weeping at mechanical seal face on P-101A during
routine rounds. Inspection confirms minor seal deviation — non-conformance
against OEM seal-flush specification. Seal flush line pressure reading low.
Temporary tightening performed; seal replacement scheduled for next planned
shutdown window. Vibration re-checked: 2.6 mm/s RMS outboard bearing, further
increase from January reading. Flagged for Inspection Engineer follow-up.
