INCIDENT REPORT
Date: Aug 2, 2024
Equipment: Pump P-101A
Classification: Mechanical seal leak / breakdown (near-miss for fire risk)
Reported By: Shift Engineer
Regulatory Notification: DGMS

At approximately 0400 hrs, a mechanical seal failure occurred on P-101A,
resulting in a process fluid leak and spray at the seal housing. The pump
tripped automatically and was isolated. No ignition source was present at
the time, avoiding a potential fire near-miss, however the location is
within 15 meters of a designated hot-work zone, raising the severity
classification. Standby pump P-101B was brought online with no process
interruption. Investigation found progressive bearing degradation and seal
weep recorded in maintenance logs since January 2024 that were not
escalated with sufficient urgency. Plant Manager has requested a formal
root-cause analysis and review of the vibration-monitoring escalation
threshold for rotating equipment.
